What is UnivArTech?

Welcome to UnivArTech 2025

“Art in motion, Gaming in action”

UnivArTech is a premier artistic and technological competition designed to inspire creativity and innovation among students from across Morocco. It is an annual event that serves as a hub for emerging digital talents to showcase their skills in gaming, digital art, and multimedia production.

In the 2025 edition, we spotlight GAMING—a thriving creative medium that blends storytelling, design, and cutting-edge technology to deliver immersive experiences. From video game concepts to digital animations, we invite participants to reimagine what’s possible in the world of gaming and beyond.

What You’ll Find at UnivArTech:

Short Films & Documentaries

Showcase your storytelling skills through engaging short films and documentaries.

Digital Art

Create and exhibit stunning visuals, from photography to NFTs.

Gaming

Bring your interactive gaming concepts to life in our competition.

Animation

Unleash your creativity with animated shorts and experiences.

Audio (Music & Podcasts)

Express yourself through music compositions or engaging podcasts.

Call for Submissions

We are seeking entries for short films, digital art, and gaming projects that challenge the norms and celebrate creativity. Categories include:

  • Short Films: Showcase your storytelling prowess.
  • Digital Art: Submit your best visual creations, from photography to NFTs.
  • Gaming: Innovate and design the next interactive experience.

Organization

UnivARTech 2025 is organized by Creative Minds, an interdisciplinary team committed to merging art, technology, and innovation. We aim to foster creativity, collaboration, and global impact.
